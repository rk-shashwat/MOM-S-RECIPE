MOM'S RECIPES
Mango Lassi

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup Ripe Mango Pulp (fresh or canned)
  2. 1 cup Plain Yogurt (dahi)
  3. 1/2 cup Cold Milk
  4. 2–3 tsp Sugar (adjust to taste)
  5. A pinch of Cardamom Powder (optional)
  6. Ice cubes (optional)
  7. Chopped Nuts or Mint Leaves for garnish (optional)

Step-by-Step Recipe

  1. Prepare Mango: Peel and chop the ripe mangoes, then blend into a smooth pulp if using fresh mango.
  2. Mix Yogurt: Whisk the yogurt until smooth and creamy.
  3. Add Mango and Milk: Combine mango pulp, yogurt, and cold milk in a blender.
  4. Add Sweetener and Flavor: Add sugar and cardamom powder, blend until frothy.
  5. Chill: Add ice cubes or refrigerate for some time.
  6. Serve: Pour into glasses and garnish with chopped nuts or mint leaves if desired. Serve fresh.
