MOM'S RECIPES

Set Dosa

Ingredients

  1. Raw Rice (Dosa Rice):2 cups
  2. Urad Dal (Split/Whole): 1½ cup
  3. Flattened Rice (Poha/Beaten Rice): 1/4 cup to1½ cup (thick or thin)
  4. Fenugreek Seeds (Methi):1½ to 1 tsp
  5. Water: For soaking and grinding
  6. Salt: To taste (approx. 1 tsp)
  7. Oil/Ghee: For roasting

Step-by-Step Recipe

  1. Soak: Rinse raw rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds together in water. Soak for 3–5 hours. In a separate bowl, wash and soak the poha for 30 minutes.
  2. Grind: Drain the water from the soaked ingredients. Grind the rice-dal-methi mixture along with the soaked poha into a very smooth, fluffy paste, adding water as needed.
  3. Ferment: Transfer the batter to a large vessel. Mix well with your hands, cover, and let it ferment in a warm place for 8–10 hours, or until doubled and bubbly.
  4. Prepare: Once fermented, gently mix the batter (do not overmix or lose the air). Add salt. The batter should be slightly thick, not as thin as regular dosa batter..
  5. Cook: Heat a non-stick or cast-iron tawa (griddle). Pour a ladleful of batter and spread it gently, keeping it thick, about 4-5 inches in diameter.
  6. Steam: Drizzle 1 tsp oil/ghee around the edges. Cover with a lid and cook on medium-low heat for about 2 minutes. The dosa is ready when the top is cooked/steamed and the bottom is light golden.
  7. Serve: Remove the dosa without flipping (or flip for a few seconds if desired). Serve hot
