MOM'S RECIPES
Sweet Lassi

Ingredients

  1. 2 cups Plain Yogurt (dahi)
  2. 1/2 cup Cold Water or Milk
  3. 3–4 tbsp Sugar (or to taste)
  4. A pinch of Cardamom Powder (optional)
  5. Ice cubes (optional)
  6. Chopped Nuts for garnish (optional)

Step-by-Step Recipe

  1. Prepare Yogurt: Whisk the yogurt until smooth and creamy.
  2. Add Liquid: Add cold water or milk to adjust consistency.
  3. Add Sweetness: Mix in sugar and cardamom powder (if using).
  4. Blend: Use a hand blender or whisk to make it frothy.
  5. Chill: Add ice cubes or refrigerate for some time.
  6. Serve: Pour into a glass, garnish with chopped nuts if desired, and serve fresh.
