Butter Chicken

Ingredients

Chicken Marinade:

  1. 500g (1.1 lbs) boneless chicken thighs, cut into 2-inch pieces
  2. 3 tbsp thick yogurt or Hung Curd
  3. 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic paste
  4. 1 tsp Kashmiri red chili powder or paprika
  5. 1 tsp Garam masala
  6. 1 tsp Lemon juice
  7. Salt to taste

Sauce (Makhani Gravy):

  1. 2 tbsp Butter + 1 tbsp Oil
  2. 1 cup Tomato puree or passata (approx. 3 large tomatoes)
  3. 1 large Onion, finely chopped
  4. 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic paste
  5. 10-12 Cashew nuts (soaked in hot water for 15 mins)
  6. 1-2 tsp Kashmiri red chili powder (adjust for heat)
  7. 1 tsp Garam masala
  8. 1 tbsp Kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves), crushed
  9. 1/4 cup Heavy cream or fresh cream
  10. 1 tsp Sugar or honey (optional, to balance acidity)
  11. 1/2 cup Water

Step-by-Step Recipe

  1. Marinate the Chicken: Combine chicken with all marinade ingredients. Marinate in the fridge for at least 30 minutes to overnight.
  2. Cook the Chicken:Heat butter and oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Sear the chicken pieces until lightly browned and cooked through (about 3-4 minutes per side). Set aside.
  3. Prepare the Sauce Base: In the same pan, add a little more butter. Sauté onions, ginger, garlic, and soaked cashews until the onions are soft and golden.
  4. Add Tomatoes and Spices: Add the tomato puree, Kashmiri red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the mixture thickens and the oil starts to separate.
  5. Blend the Sauce: Allow the mixture to cool completely, then blend in a blender until very smooth. Optional: Strain the gravy for a silky texture.
  6. Simmer the Gravy: Pour the blended gravy back into the pan. Add water to adjust consistency, followed by garam masala, sugar (if using), and kasuri methi. Bring to a gentle simmer.
  7. Combine: Add the cooked chicken back into the gravy. Stir in the heavy cream and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  8. Serve: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and an extra drizzle of cream. Serve hot with naan or rice.
