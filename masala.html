Masala Dosa

Ingredients

For the Batter:

  1. 3 cups Sona Masuri rice (or idli rice)
  2. 1 cup Urad dal (skinned black gram)
  3. 2 tbsp Chana dal (split chickpeas)
  4. 1/2 tsp Methi seeds (fenugreek seeds)
  5. 1/2 cup thick Poha (flattened rice), optional
  6. Salt to taste
  7. Water to grind

For the Potato Filling (Masala):

  1. 4-5 large potatoes, boiled, peeled, and mashed
  2. 2 tbsp oil
  3. 1 tsp mustard seeds
  4. 1 tsp chana dal
  5. 1 tsp urad dal
  6. 2 onions, thinly sliced
  7. 2-3 green chilies, choppedA few curry leaves
  8. 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  9. Salt to taste
  10. 1/2 cup water

Step-by-Step Recipe

  1. Soak: Rinse the rice, urad dal, chana dal, and methi seeds separately. Soak the rice for 4 hours and the dal/methi mixture for 2 hours. Add the poha to the rice while soaking.
  2. Grind:Drain the water. Grind the dal mixture into a very smooth, fluffy paste, adding water as needed. Grind the rice and poha into a slightly coarse paste.
  3. Combine & Ferment: Mix both batters together in a large bowl, add salt, and mix well with hands. Cover and let it ferment in a warm place for 8-10 hours or until it doubles in volume.
