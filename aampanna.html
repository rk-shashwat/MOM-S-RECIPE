MOM'S RECIPES
Aam Panna

Ingredients

  1. 2 Raw Mangoes (kaccha aam)
  2. 3–4 tbsp Sugar or Jaggery (to taste)
  3. 1/2 tsp Roasted Cumin Powder
  4. A pinch of Black Salt
  5. A pinch of Regular Salt
  6. 6–8 Fresh Mint Leaves (optional)
  7. 2–3 cups Cold Water
  8. Ice cubes (optional)

Step-by-Step Recipe

  1. Cook Mangoes: Boil or pressure cook the raw mangoes until soft.
  2. Cool & Peel: Let them cool, then peel the skin and remove the pulp.
  3. Prepare Pulp: Mash or blend the pulp into a smooth paste.
  4. Mix Ingredients: Add sugar or jaggery, roasted cumin powder, black salt, and regular salt.
  5. Add Water: Pour cold water and mix well to adjust consistency.
  6. Add Mint (Optional): Crush mint leaves and mix for extra freshness.
  7. Serve: Pour into a glass, add ice cubes, and serve chilled.
