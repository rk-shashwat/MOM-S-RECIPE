Lucknowi (Awadhi) Biryani

Ingredients

For Marination (Meat & Yakhni):

  1. 2 Lbs Mutton (with bones) or Chicken (cut into medium pieces)
  2. 1/2 cup Thick Yogurt/Curd, whisked
  3. 3 TBSP Ginger-Garlic Paste
  4. 1 tsp Red Chili Powder (or yellow chili powder)
  5. 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  6. 1 tsp Garam Masala Powder (cardamom/mace)
  7. 1 cup Fried Onions (Barista)
  8. 1/4 cup Ghee
  9. 1 tsp Rose water & 1 tsp Kewra water
  10. Salt to taste

Rice Preparation:

  1. 3 cups long-grain Basmati Rice (soaked for 30-60 mins)
  2. 1 Bay leaf, 3-4 Cloves, 2-3 Green Cardamom, 1-inch Cinnamon
  3. 1 tsp Shahi Jeera (Caraway Seeds)
  4. Salt (water should taste like sea water)

Layering:

  1. S1/2 tsp Saffron soaked in 1/4 cup warm milk/water
  2. Ghee for drizzling
  3. Mint leaves and Coriander leaves

Step-by-Step Recipe:

  1. Marination: In a large bowl, mix the mutton/chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, salt, half of the fried onions, and ghee. Marinate for at least 2 hours, or overnight for best results.
  2. Fry Onions: Slice 3 medium onions thinly and deep fry until golden brown. Set aside.
  3. Cook the Meat (Yakhni): In a heavy-bottomed pan (handi), heat the oil. Add the marinated meat and cook on medium-high heat for 5-7 minutes to sear. Cover and cook on low heat until the meat is 80-90% cooked and tender. If using mutton, add a little water and cook until tender.
  4. Prepare the Rice: While the meat cooks, boil a large pot of water with whole spices (bay leaf, cloves, cardamom, cinnamon), shahi jeera, and salt. Add the soaked rice. Cook until it is 70-80%
