Masala Chai

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup Water
  2. 1 cup Milk
  3. 1–2 tsp Tea leaves
  4. 1–2 tsp Sugar (to taste)
  5. 1 inch Ginger (crushed)
  6. 2–3 Green cardamom pods (crushed)
  7. 1 small cinnamon stick
  8. 2 cloves
  9. 2–3 black peppercorns

Step-by-Step Recipe

  1. Prepare Base: In a saucepan, add water and bring it to a boil.
  2. Add Spices:Add crushed ginger, cardamom, cinnamon, cloves, and peppercorns. Let it simmer for 2–3 minutes to release flavors.
  3. Add Tea:Add tea leaves and boil for another 1–2 minutes until the color deepens.
  4. Add Milk and Sugar:Pour in the milk and add sugar. Stir well.
  5. Boil:Let the chai come to a gentle boil for 2–3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Strain:Turn off the heat and strain into cups using a strainer.
  7. Serve:Serve hot and enjoy aromatic masala chai.
