MOM'S RECIPES

Roti

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  1. 2 cups Whole Wheat Flour (Atta/Chapati flour)
  2. ~1 cup Warm Water (adjust for dough consistency)
  3. 1 tsp Oil or Ghee (optional, makes it softer)
  4. ½ tsp Salt (optional)

Step-by-Step Recipe:

  1. Prepare the Dough: Mix the whole wheat flour and salt (if using) in a large bowl. Gradually add warm water, mixing with your hand, adding a little at a time.
  2. Knead: Add oil/ghee (if using). Knead the dough with your knuckles, stretching and folding until it is smooth, soft, and non-sticky. The dough should leave an impression when pressed.
  3. Rest: Cover the dough with a damp cloth or bowl and let it rest for at least 20–30 minutes to develop the gluten and make the roti soft.
  4. Divide and Roll: Divide the dough into 8–10 equal, smooth, round balls. Flatten them into small discs. Dust a ball in flour, place on a rolling board, and use a rolling pin to roll it into a thin 6–8 inch circle.
  5. Cook (Tawa): Heat a flat pan or tawa on medium-high heat. Place the rolled roti on the hot tawa.
  6. Flip: When bubbles start to appear (about 30 seconds), flip the roti.
  7. Puff: Cook the other side for another 30 seconds. You can press the edges gently with a spatula or clean cloth to encourage it to puff up.
  8. Finish: Remove from heat and, if desired, apply ghee or butter for extra softness.
