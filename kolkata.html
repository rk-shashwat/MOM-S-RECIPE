Kolkata Biryani

Ingredients

  1. Chicken: 500g (cut into medium pieces).
  2. Marinade: 1.5 tsp salt, 1 tsp turmeric, 2 tsp red chili powder.
  3. Gravy Base: 3 tbsp oil, 2 small onions (cubed), 2 medium tomatoes (cubed), 1" ginger (chopped), 10-11 garlic cloves.
  4. Spices:2 tsp coriander powder, 1 tsp Kashmiri chili powder, 3/4 tsp garam masala, 2 green chilies, 15 curry leaves.
  5. Finishing: 3 tbsp curd/yogurt, 4 tbsp coriander leaves.

Step-by-Step Recipe

  1. Marination: Combine chicken with salt, turmeric, and chili powder. Let it rest for at least 15-20 minutes.
  2. Sauté Aromatics:Heat oil in a wok. Sauté onions, green chilies, ginger, and garlic until onions turn soft and golden.
  3. Cook Tomatoes: Add tomatoes and curry leaves, cooking until the tomatoes turn mushy.
  4. Add Spices: While the meat cooks, boil a large pot of water with whole spices (bay leaf, cloves, cardamom, cinnamon), shahi jeera, and salt. Add the soaked rice. Cook until it is 70-80%
  5. Cook Chicken: Add the marinated chicken and cook on high heat until it turns white, then lower the heat and cook until partially done.
  6. Simmer:Stir in the curd and water. Cover and simmer until the chicken is fully cooked and tender.
  7. Garnish:: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve
