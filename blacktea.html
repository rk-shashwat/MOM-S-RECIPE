MOM'S RECIPES
Black Tea

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup Water
  2. 1 tsp Tea leaves (or 1 tea bag)
  3. 1–2 tsp Sugar (optional, to taste)
  4. 1 tsp Lemon juice (optional)

Step-by-Step Recipe

  1. Boil Water: In a saucepan, bring water to a boil.
  2. Add Tea: Add tea leaves (or tea bag) and let it boil for 2–3 minutes.
  3. Add Sugar (Optional): Add sugar if desired and stir well.
  4. Strain: Turn off the heat and strain the tea into a cup (skip if using tea bag).
  5. Add Lemon (Optional): Add a little lemon juice for extra flavor.
  6. Serve: Serve hot and enjoy your black tea.
