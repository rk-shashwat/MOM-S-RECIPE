Bhel Puri

Ingredients

  1. Base: 3 cups Puffed Rice (Murmura), dry roasted until crisp
  2. Veggies: 1 small onion (finely chopped), 1 small potato (boiled & diced), 1 tomato (deseeded & chopped)
  3. Chutneys: 3 tbsp Tamarind (Sweet) Chutney, 1-2 tbsp Green (Mint-Coriander) Chutney, 1 tbsp Garlic Chutney
  4. Crunch & Garnish: 1/2 cup Nylon Sev, 1/4 cup Roasted Peanuts, 4-5 crushed Papdis, 2 tbsp Coriander leaves (chopped)
  5. Spices & Tang: 1 tsp Chaat Masala, 1/2 tsp Red Chilli Powder, 1 tsp Lemon juice, Salt to taste

Step-by-Step Recipe:

  1. Prepare Ingredients: Finely chop the vegetables, boil and cube the potato, and roast the puffed rice if it's not crunchy.
  2. Combine Dry Ingredients: In a large bowl, combine the roasted puffed rice, chopped onions, potatoes, tomatoes, roasted peanuts, and crushed papdis.
  3. Add Chutneys and Spices: Add the green chutney, sweet tamarind chutney, and red garlic chutney. Sprinkle the chaat masala, red chili powder, and salt.
  4. Mix and Garnish: Drizzle the lemon juice and add the nylon sev. Mix everything quickly and thoroughly with a large spoon.
  5. Serve: Garnish with extra sev and coriander leaves. Serve immediately in bowls to prevent it from turning soggy.
