MOM'S RECIPES
Pudina Sharbat

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup Fresh Mint Leaves (pudina)
  2. 3–4 tbsp Sugar (or to taste)
  3. 1–2 tbsp Lemon Juice
  4. A pinch of Black Salt
  5. 2–3 cups Cold Water
  6. Ice cubes (optional)

Step-by-Step Recipe

  1. Prepare Mint: Wash the mint leaves thoroughly.
  2. Blend: Add mint leaves, sugar, lemon juice, and a little water to a blender and blend into a smooth paste.
  3. Strain: Strain the mixture to remove the pulp for a smooth drink.
  4. Mix: Add cold water and a pinch of black salt, then mix well.
  5. Chill: Add ice cubes or refrigerate for some time.
  6. Serve: Pour into a glass and serve chilled.
