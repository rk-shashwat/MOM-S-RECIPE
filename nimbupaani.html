MOM'S RECIPES
Nimbu Paani

Ingredients

  1. 2 cups Cold Water
  2. 1–2 Fresh Lemons
  3. 2–3 tsp Sugar (or to taste)
  4. A pinch of Black Salt
  5. A pinch of Regular Salt
  6. Ice cubes (optional)

Step-by-Step Recipe

  1. Prepare Base: Take cold water in a glass or jug.
  2. Add Lemon: Squeeze fresh lemon juice into the water.
  3. Add Ingredients: Add sugar, black salt, and regular salt.
  4. Mix Well: Stir until the sugar dissolves completely.
  5. Adjust Taste: Adjust sweetness or salt as per your preference.
  6. Serve: Add ice cubes and serve chilled.
