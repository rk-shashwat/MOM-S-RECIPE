Watermelon Juice

Ingredients

  1. 2–3 cups Fresh Watermelon (seedless, cubed)
  2. 1–2 tsp Sugar or Honey (optional, to taste)
  3. A pinch of Black Salt or Regular Salt (optional)
  4. 1–2 tsp Lemon Juice (optional, for freshness)
  5. Ice cubes (optional)

Step-by-Step Recipe

  1. Prepare Watermelon: Cut the watermelon into cubes and remove seeds if present.
  2. Blend: Add the watermelon cubes to a blender and blend until smooth.
  3. Strain (Optional): Strain the juice if you prefer a smoother texture.
  4. Add Flavor: Add sugar or honey, a pinch of salt, and lemon juice if desired.
  5. Chill: Add ice cubes or refrigerate for a while to make it cold.
  6. Serve: Pour into a glass and serve fresh.
