Orange Juice

Ingredients

  1. 3–4 Fresh Oranges
  2. 1–2 tsp Sugar or Honey (optional, to taste)
  3. A pinch of Salt (optional)
  4. 1/2 cup Cold Water (optional)
  5. Ice cubes (optional)

Step-by-Step Recipe

  1. Prepare Oranges: Wash, peel, and remove seeds from the oranges.
  2. Extract Juice: Blend the orange segments in a mixer or squeeze using a juicer.
  3. Strain (Optional): Strain the juice to remove pulp if you prefer a smooth texture.
  4. Add Flavor: Add sugar or honey and a pinch of salt if desired.
  5. Adjust Consistency: Add a little cold water if the juice is too thick.
  6. Serve: Pour into a glass, add ice cubes, and serve fresh.
