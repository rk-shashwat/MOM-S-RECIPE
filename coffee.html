MOM'S RECIPES
Coffee

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup Milk
  2. 1–2 tsp Instant Coffee powder
  3. 1–2 tsp Sugar (to taste)
  4. 2 tbsp Water

Step-by-Step Recipe

  1. Prepare Coffee Base: In a cup, add coffee powder, sugar, and water.
  2. Whisk: Beat the mixture vigorously until it becomes light and frothy.
  3. Heat Milk: In a saucepan, heat the milk until hot but not boiling.
  4. Combine: Pour the hot milk over the whipped coffee mixture.
  5. Mix: Stir gently to combine the froth with the milk.
  6. Serve: Serve hot and enjoy your creamy coffee.
