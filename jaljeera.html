Jaljeera

Ingredients

  1. 2 cups Cold Water
  2. 1–2 tsp Jaljeera Powder
  3. 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
  4. A pinch of Black Salt
  5. A pinch of Roasted Cumin Powder
  6. Fresh Mint Leaves (optional)
  7. Ice cubes (optional)

Step-by-Step Recipe

  1. Prepare Base: Take cold water in a jug.
  2. Mix Spices: Add jaljeera powder, black salt, and roasted cumin powder.
  3. Add Lemon: Add fresh lemon juice for tanginess.
  4. Enhance Flavor: Crush mint leaves and add them (optional).
  5. Mix Well: Stir everything properly until well combined.
  6. Serve: Pour into a glass, add ice cubes, and serve chilled.
