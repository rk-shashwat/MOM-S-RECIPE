Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  1. Chicken: 1 kg, cut into pieces (preferably bone-in)
  2. Marinade: 1 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp salt, 2 tsp chili powder, 1/2 cup yogurt (optional)
  3. Whole Spices: 1 bay leaf, 1-inch cinnamon stick, 3-4 cloves, 3-4 cardamom pods
  4. Aromatics: 2-3 large onions (finely chopped), 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  5. Tomatoes: 2 medium tomatoes (chopped or pureed)
  6. Spices: 1 tbsp coriander powder, 1 tsp cumin powder, 1-2 tsp red chili powder, 1 tsp Garam Masala
  7. Others: 3-4 tbsp oil, 1 cup hot water, salt to taste, coriander leaves for garnish

Step-by-Step Recipe

  1. Marinate Chicken: Mix chicken with yogurt, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Set aside for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Temper Spices: Heat oil in a pan (or kadhai) over medium heat. Add whole spices (bay leaf, cinnamon, cloves, cardamom) and let them splutter.
  3. Sauté Aromatics: Add chopped onions and fry until light brown (about 10 mins). Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for 1-2 minutes until raw smell disappears.
  4. Cook Masala: Add tomatoes, red chili powder, coriander powder, and cumin powder. Cook until the tomatoes become mushy and oil starts to separate from the masala.
  5. Cook Chicken: Add the marinated chicken and fry on high heat for 5-10 minutes to sear it.
  6. Simmer: Add 1 cup hot water (optional, depending on desired gravy thickness). Cover and cook on low heat for 15-20 minutes until the chicken is tender.
  7. Finish: Stir in garam masala and garnish with coriander leaves. Serve hot with rice or roti.
