Buttermilk(Chaas)

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup Curd (Yogurt)
  2. 1/2–1 cup Water
  3. 1/2 tsp Roasted Cumin Powder
  4. Salt to taste
  5. 2 tbsp Coriander leaves (chopped)
  6. 2–3 Mint leaves (optional)
  7. A pinch of Black Salt (optional)
