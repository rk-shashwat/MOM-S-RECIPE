MOM'S RECIPES

Naan

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  1. All-purpose flour (Maida): 2 cups
  2. Instant Yeast: 1 tsp (or 1/2 tsp baking powder + 1/4 tsp baking soda)
  3. Sugar: 1 tsp
  4. Salt: 1 tsp (or to taste)
  5. Yogurt (Curd): 1/4 cup (plain/thick)
  6. Warm Water/Milk: 1/2 cup to 3/4 cup (as needed)
  7. Oil/Ghee: 1–2 tbsp

For Toppings/Cooking:

  1. Melted butter or Ghee
  2. Minced garlic (for garlic naan)
  3. Finely chopped coriander leaves (cilantro)

Step-by-Step Recipe:

  1. Activate Yeast: Mix 1 tsp yeast and 1 tsp sugar in 1/4 cup warm water. Let it sit for 10 minutes until foamy.
  2. Make the Dough: In a large bowl, mix flour, salt, yeast mixture, yogurt, and 1 tbsp oil. Knead, adding warm water/milk gradually, until a soft, smooth, and pliable dough forms (about 5-7 minutes).
  3. Rise: Grease the dough with oil, cover the bowl with a moist cloth or plastic wrap, and let it rest in a warm place for 1–2 hours until doubled in size.
  4. Shape: Punch down the dough to release air. Divide into 6-8 equal balls.
  5. Roll: On a floured surface, roll one ball into an oval or circle, about 1 to 4-inch thick.
  6. Cook:Heat a cast-iron skillet over high heat. Brush one side of the rolled dough with water and place that side down on the hot pan. When bubbles appear (1-2 mins), flip the skillet upside down directly over the open flame to char the top, or cook until golden brown on both sides.
  7. Finish: Remove from heat and immediately brush with butter, garlic and coriander.
