Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients

  1. Paneer: 250g, cubed
  2. Base: 1 large onion (chopped), 3-4 medium tomatoes (chopped), 10-15 cashews (soaked)
  3. Aromatics: 1-inch ginger, 4-5 garlic cloves, 1-2 green chilies (optional)
  4. Fats: 2 tbsp butter, 1 tbsp oil
  5. Spices: 1 tsp Kashmiri red chili powder (for color), 1/2 tsp turmeric, 1/2 tsp garam masala, 1 tsp cumin seeds
  6. Finishers: 2 tbsp fresh cream, 1 tbsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves), 1 tsp sugar (optional)

Step-by-Step Recipe

  1. Prepare the Base: Heat 1 tbsp oil/butter in a pan. Add ginger, garlic, onions, and cashews. Sauté until onions are translucent. Add tomatoes and cook until they are soft and mushy.
  2. Blend Allow the mixture to cool, then blend into a smooth paste. Add a little water if needed.
  3. Cook Gravy: Heat the remaining butter in the same pan. Add cumin seeds. Pour in the blended paste and cook for 3-4 minutes until oil separates from the edges.
  4. Add Spices: Add Kashmiri red chili powder, turmeric, and salt. Mix well and cook for 1 minute.
  5. Simmer: Add 1/2 cup water to adjust consistency. Add paneer cubes, sugar, and kasuri methi. Cover and simmer for 5 minutes on low heat.
  6. Finish: Stir in the fresh cream and turn off the heat. Garnish with coriander and serve hot.
